Scree Factory Alpine Endurance

Full Stack: Express, Mongoose, MongoDB, Node, Bootstrap

This gem takes all the knowledge and experience in my head that I use to formulate workouts for runners & climbers based on their imbalances so we can correct them to maximize performance and minimize injuries, then uses pages of JS logic to recreate that process and instantly produce perfect custom workouts!
